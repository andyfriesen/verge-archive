*** Hatchet's Battle System Tutorial ***

I finally decided to write a badly needed battle system tutorial. I will be writing a battle system from scratch, and writing down how I did it as I go along. This is just a bare bones one - don't expect fancy spell effects or great graphics or anything. However, it will contain a working stat, item, magic, and attack system, and support for multiple party members and enemies. I'm hoping that someone will make use of this.

This battle system is loosely based on the original Dragon Warrior's system, since that was the easiest to program. However, unlike DW, it will have "time" in battles. 

The first thing we need to do when writing a battle system is create a stats system. So let's do that!
